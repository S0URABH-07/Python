Interview Questions
    1. What is a loop?
            A loop repeatedly executes a block of code until a condition is no longer satisfied or all items in a sequence have been processed.

    2. Difference between for and while?
            for Loop	while Loop
            Used when the number of iterations is known	Used when the number of iterations depends on a condition
            Often used with sequences or range()	Runs until the condition becomes False
            
    3. What is the purpose of break?
            It exits the loop immediately.

    4. What is the purpose of continue?
            It skips the current iteration and continues with the next one.

    5. What is an infinite loop?   
            A loop that never ends because its condition always remains True.
