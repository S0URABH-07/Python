Interview Questions
    1. What is a list?
            A list is an ordered, mutable collection that can store multiple values of different data types.

    2. Difference between append() and extend()?
            append() adds a single item.
            extend() adds all items from another iterable.
    
    3. Difference between remove() and pop()?
            remove(value) removes by value.
            pop(index) removes by index and returns the removed item.
            
    4. Why are lists called mutable?
            Because you can add, remove, or modify elements after the list is created.
